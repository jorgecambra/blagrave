What can we say about Blagrave House? Well... this was our home for one year. For the three terms of the 2000-2001 year while studying at the University of Reading, to be precise. Parties, lots of dinners, sunbathing in our lovely garden (when the weather was not too bad, of course ;-) ) ...and some other things.

This Web Page tries to remember those fantastic moments that we shared there, and all the great and fantastic people that we have met.

Why a picture of our W.C.? Well, I think everybody will remember our fantastic toilets that were "supposed" to be redecorated over Christmas. I'm still waiting because I believe in the Hall Office and in their concern for our happiness and comfort.
