...but here is an explanation of how to get here:

If you are comming by train Go to Reading, take the 63 bus from the station and get off in Bulmershe Court

If you are comming by car (or bike) You've gotta take the M4 and get off in Junction 10, taking the A329 direction to Reading. Once there, keep going untill the last exit (Reading and Sutton Business Park). Once you've left the A329 take the first roundabout on the right, go straight on, second roundabout on the right again, 5 meters and the road turns on the right. Follow THE DRIVE road, then WHITEGATES road untill a roundabout with CHURCH ROAD. Turn on the right and drive untill the next roudabout. Turn there on the left getting in WOODLANDS AVENUE and drive a few meters. You will see on your right the "Bulmershe Court" and "University of Reading" signs. Here your are!
